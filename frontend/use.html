How to use?

For a WhatsApp analysis, export your WhatsApp conversation using the steps on this page and upload it using the button in Home page. We will not be able to view or save your conversations.

1. On your WhatsApp app, go to your chat with someone and press Three dots

2. Press “More Chat”

3. Press “Export Chat”

4. Select “Without Media”

5. Get your ZIP file & upload

save it to your phone files, & then upload it using our the upload button in Home page.
